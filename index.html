Our Story

Beauty Born

Sandra Luxe was born in the heart of Kampala, Uganda — a vision to make luxury beauty accessible to every African woman. Our founder believed that every woman deserves to feel radiant, seen, and celebrated.

We craft every product with love, sourcing natural ingredients from Uganda's rich landscapes. From shea butter harvested in the north to the finest botanical extracts — nature meets science in every bottle.
